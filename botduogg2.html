aatroxahri Ahri and Aatrox can be made into a decent bot lane duo. There are several reasons why this combo can work. The combination of the champions have an insane all-in, if you can hit the Ahri charm(E), you can follow up with the (Q) and (E) for maximum damage as well as the Aatrox Q1, Q2, Q3 for a definitive kill. If Aatrox hits the (W) and the enemy champ does not escape using the side of the (W) or flashing or dashing out, you can then follow up with a Ahri charm into full combo as well. However there are a few conditions that need to be met for this duo to work, first, the Aatrox needs to be a “good Aatrox” in which all or at least 2(Q)’s needs to be hit consistently. Secondly, the Ahri needs to be able to hit the skill shot for the charm when given the opportunity.

aatroxakali Aatrox and Akali is not a very viable bot lane duo. Due to the short range of the duo it would be very hard to do anything except get poked down before six. However, there can be situations where the duo are capable of working in the bot lane. If the enemy bot lane don't respect the early close range power you have and walks to forward, the Aatrox (Q)’s and the chase potential from Akali’s level 2 from the passive autos, (Q) damage and (E) chase especially in a long lane can get a early kill if played well. If this can be done, this lane can be snowballed if the enemy continues not to respect the power. Otherwise, you would be hard pressed to do anything until 6, where the Akali and Aatrox massively spike and can all in to get a kill. However, the cooldowns for the all in is very long and the duo would most likely behind from the harass that the team receives early game. For these reasons, this not not a very viable bot lane duo, but can work in certain situations.

aatroxalistar Aatrox and Alistar is a barley viable bot lane duo. Due to the extremely short range the duo is, it is easy to be harassed during early game, however, due to the healing from both the Aatrox and Alistar passives it can be managed. This duo is very situational and could be played against a more squishy and short ranged enemy duo. The stun (E) and dash knock up (W-Q) from the alistar can help the Aatrox get a full combo and kill one of the opponents. The level 6 potential would also be very high due to the engage and healing/tankiness. This could help start a snowball and make a “raid boss”/unkillable Aatrox if you could amass a couple of kills and pick good fights early. The tankiness and teamfight potential of the Aatrox and Alistar can be great for winning skirmishes and teamfights in the later stages of the game make it a situational, barely viable bot lane duo.
